Security

Enterprise-Grade Security

Your trust is our priority. Learn how we protect your sensitive documents at every step.

Our Security Commitments

We Love PDF is built from the ground up with a privacy-first architecture. We follow strict industry standards to ensure that your documents remain secure, private, and confidential.

1. Local Sandbox Processing

Whenever you merge, split, delete pages, rotate, watermark, or convert files using our browser-based tools, your files are processed inside your browser's sandboxed memory. No files are transmitted over the web, guaranteeing absolute data sovereignty.

2. Automated Server Deletion

When you use our backend tools (like OCR or AI templates), files are stored temporarily on our servers in an encrypted volume. Files are automatically and permanently deleted 1 hour after processing. There is no manual or automatic archiving of customer files.

3. End-to-End Encryption

All communications between your browser and our servers are encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS 1.3/HTTPS). Data on our temporary servers is encrypted at rest using AES-256 standards.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We respect user rights. We do not track document content, index private information, or compile personal profiles. You retain 100% ownership of your files.

Cookies Policy

Cookies Statement

How we use cookies and local storage to provide a fast, reliable web workspace.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. Local Storage is a similar mechanism that stores key-value settings on your local machine.

How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

We Love PDF uses cookies and local storage for the following essential purposes:

  • Session Authentication: If you are a Pro user, essential cookies keep you logged in while you navigate our tools.
  • Preferences: We use local storage to remember your active theme, UI state, last selected tool, and category choices. This avoids resetting your configuration on every page refresh.
  • Analytics: We may use cookie tokens to analyze aggregate visitor statistics to prioritize which PDF tools we improve next.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can adjust your browser's security settings to restrict, delete, or block cookies. However, blocking all cookies may prevent you from logging in to your premium account.

What is the AI PDF Summarizer?

The WeLovePDF AI PDF Summarizer is a smart tool designed to analyze long PDF documents, reports, research papers, and books, extracting key points, core takeaways, and main arguments instantly. It saves you hours of reading by providing structured summaries in seconds.

How it Works

  1. Upload Document: Upload your PDF file. The text is extracted directly in your browser.
  2. Process Text: The extracted text is securely processed using high-performance AI models to summarize the content.
  3. Download Summary: Get your structured outline, takeaways, and bulleted summary as a new PDF.

Key Benefits

  • Save Time: Turn 100-page reports into 1-page summaries in seconds.
  • Privacy First: Your documents are encrypted during transit, processed in memory, and never cached or used to train models.
  • Structured Output: Outlines are presented with logical headers and clear bullet points.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data shared when using the AI Summarizer?

No. WeLovePDF maintains strict data security. Your files are processed temporarily in memory to generate the summary and are permanently deleted within 1 hour. We never sell your data or train AI models with it.

Free users can summarize documents up to 20MB. Pro users can process documents up to 200MB.
